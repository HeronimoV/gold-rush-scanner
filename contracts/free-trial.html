2. TRIAL PERIOD

2.1 Duration. The trial period begins on the Effective Date and ends fourteen (14) calendar days later ("Trial Period").

2.2 Platform Access. During the Trial Period, Client shall receive full access to the Social Prospector platform, including all features available under the Gold Plan subscription tier.

2.3 No Upfront Fee. Client shall not be charged any subscription fee during the Trial Period. The sole compensation to Provider during the Trial Period is the Revenue Share described in Section 3.

3. REVENUE SHARE

3.1 Revenue Share Rate. Client agrees to pay Provider ten percent (10%) of gross revenue collected from any customer, client, or project that:

(a) Was identified, discovered, or sourced through the Social Prospector platform during the Trial Period ("Platform Lead"); AND

(b) Results in a signed contract, completed transaction, or payment received by Client within ninety (90) days of the Trial Period end date.

3.2 Lead Attribution. A lead shall be considered a "Platform Lead" if it appeared on the Client's Social Prospector dashboard during the Trial Period and Client subsequently contacted, engaged with, or responded to that lead through any channel.

3.3 Reporting Obligation. Client shall provide Provider with a written report within thirty (30) days of the Trial Period end date, and monthly thereafter for the 90-day attribution window, identifying:

(a) Which Platform Leads were contacted;

(b) Which Platform Leads resulted in signed contracts or completed jobs;

(c) The gross revenue received from each closed Platform Lead.

3.4 Payment. Revenue share payments are due within fifteen (15) days of each reporting period. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

3.5 Audit Rights. Provider reserves the right to request reasonable documentation (invoices, contracts, payment records) to verify reported revenue. Client shall provide such documentation within ten (10) business days of request.

3.6 Minimum Platform Fee. If Client closes one or more Platform Leads during or after the Trial Period (within the 90-day attribution window) and does not convert to a paid subscription, Client shall pay a minimum platform fee of $500.00 in addition to any revenue share owed.

4. CONVERSION TO PAID SUBSCRIPTION

4.1 At the conclusion of the Trial Period, Client may elect to convert to a paid subscription plan (Gold or Platinum) by providing written notice to Provider.

4.2 If Client converts to a paid subscription within seven (7) days of the Trial Period end date, the minimum platform fee described in Section 3.6 shall be waived.

4.3 If Client does not convert to a paid subscription, platform access shall be terminated at the end of the Trial Period.

5. NO GUARANTEE OF RESULTS

5.1 Provider makes NO representations, warranties, or guarantees regarding:

(a) The number, quality, or accuracy of leads generated by the Platform;

(b) Whether any leads will result in customer contact, engagement, or response;

(c) Whether any leads will result in signed contracts, completed projects, or revenue;

(d) Any specific business outcomes, revenue figures, or return on investment.

5.2 The Platform is an intelligence tool. It identifies publicly available discussions and scores them for potential buying intent. Client is solely responsible for all outreach, engagement, sales, and closing activities.

5.3 No Refunds. Client acknowledges that the Trial Period is provided at no upfront cost and that no refunds or credits shall be issued for any reason, including but not limited to dissatisfaction with lead quality, lead volume, or business outcomes.

5.4 Lead Data. Leads identified by the Platform are sourced from publicly available information. Provider does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or currentness of any lead data. Client is solely responsible for verifying lead information before taking action.

6. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

6.1 Client shall designate at least one authorized user to access the Platform during the Trial Period.

6.2 Client shall make reasonable, good-faith efforts to review and act upon leads identified during the Trial Period.

6.3 Client shall accurately and honestly report all closed Platform Leads and associated revenue as required by Section 3.3.

6.4 Intentional underreporting or concealment of closed Platform Leads shall constitute a material breach of this Agreement and entitle Provider to liquidated damages of $5,000.00 per unreported lead, in addition to the revenue share owed.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 Client shall not disclose Platform features, pricing, algorithms, scoring methodology, or any proprietary information to any third party.

7.2 Client shall not share dashboard access credentials with anyone outside of designated authorized users.

7.3 Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for five (5) years.

8.2 Client acquires no ownership interest in the Platform or any intellectual property of Provider through this Agreement.

9. NON-COMPETE

9.1 During the Trial Period and for one (1) year following, Client shall not develop, purchase, license, or use any substantially similar lead intelligence platform for the same industry and geographic area covered by this Agreement.
